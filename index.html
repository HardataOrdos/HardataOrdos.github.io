About Us

Hardata(Internet) Technology Ltd. was established in Ordos, Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, China, in 2019. We are deeply involved in the market, focusing on geospatial data acquisition, analysis, and application, providing an all-in-one solution to our clients.

OUR MISSION

We are devoted improving asset spatial management efficiency and lowering management costs for enterprises and organizations by geospatial data, quantitative and Geo-AI models.

OUR TEAM

Core part of our team are from The World Bank, The George Washington University, Peking University and Netease.

OUR TECH

We offer programming Technology, Artificial Intelligence technology, GIS technology, remote sensing technology, big data processing technology and other technologies to provide our customers best quality services.

Our Services

We provide our clients a wide range of geospatial data services and customized software development services.

Data acquisition

We offer spatial data collection services such as DEM, DTM, DLG, and DOM, collecting by drone.

Software

We are offering customized industry-grade software to our customers based on our platforms.

Consultation

We are offering geospatial-related consultation services such as GIS modeling and SLAM.
